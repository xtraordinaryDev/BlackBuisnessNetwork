Membership

A: BBN members receive access to networking events, business development resources, mentorship programs, the BBN Business Directory, and our supportive community.

A: The BBN Builders Club is our donation-based membership program with added benefits, including exclusive event access, recognition, and impact reports. It allows members to support BBN’s mission further while enjoying special perks.

A: Basic membership is free, but joining the BBN Builders Club requires a monthly or one-time donation, offering additional benefits.
