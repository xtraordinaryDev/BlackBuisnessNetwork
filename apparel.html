Coming Soon – Launching Q3 2025

BBN is thrilled to announce the upcoming launch of our exclusive apparel line, designed to empower and unite our community. Our collection will include high-quality, affordable items such as T-shirts, hats, sweatshirts, and more, allowing supporters to represent BBN proudly and accessibly.

Each purchase supports BBN’s mission to foster economic growth and community engagement for Black-owned businesses across Minnesota. Stay tuned for updates as we approach the official launch in Q3 2025!
