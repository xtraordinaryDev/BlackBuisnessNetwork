BBN Builders Club (Donation Membership Program)

The BBN Builders Club is a community of monthly donors committed to uplifting Black-owned businesses in Minnesota. Your recurring donation supports our mission through mentorship, workshops, and community events. Join us in building a stronger future.

Supporter

Receive updates on the impact of your contribution and its role in supporting BBN initiatives.

Supporter ($5/month): Receive regular updates on BBN initiatives and a thank-you mention in the quarterly newsletter.

Join as a Supporter

To join the BBN Builders monthly giving program, select the 'Monthly' option on the ESNDC donation page and include the following in the Supporter section.

Builder

Gain recognition in our newsletters and priority listing in the business directory.

Builder ($10/month): Includes all Supporter benefits plus a personalized thank-you email and priority access to event tickets.

Join as a Builder

To join the BBN Builders monthly giving program, select the 'Monthly' option on the ESNDC donation page and include the following in the Builder section.

Leader

Receive early access to event tickets and invitations to exclusive gatherings.

Leader ($25/month): Includes all Builder benefits, special mention on the BBN website, and exclusive BBN merchandise (T-shirt).

Join as a Leader

Generational Wealth
