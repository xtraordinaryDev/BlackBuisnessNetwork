As a sponsor, you’ll be directly supporting workshops, mentorship, and community events that foster growth for Black-owned businesses in Minnesota. Sponsors also receive exclusive BBN apparel and recognition across our platforms.

Sponsorship Levels

Explore the unique benefits available at each sponsorship level. Each tier offers special recognition, and select levels include exclusive BBN apparel as an added incentive.

Community Sponsor ($100–$400)

  • Website Visibility: Name listed on the BBN website under “Community Sponsors.”
  • Social Media Promotion: One shout-out on BBN’s social media platforms.
  • Newsletter Recognition: Name included in BBN’s digital newsletter.
  • Christmas Party: 1 Ticket to the Christmas Party.

Builder Sponsor ($500–$900)

  • Website Visibility: Medium-sized logo featured on the BBN website.
  • Event Materials: Logo included on promotional materials for one BBN event.
  • Social Media Promotion: Featured post highlighting the sponsor during the campaign period.
  • Christmas Party: 2 Tickets to the Christmas Party.

Visionary Sponsor ($1,000+)

  • Website Visibility: Large logo featured prominently on the BBN website.
  • Event Materials: Logo included on all promotional materials for major BBN events, including the Business Fair/Silent Auction.
  • Social Media Promotion: Featured post and additional mentions during the sponsorship period.
  • Christmas Party: 4 Tickets to the Christmas Party.
  • Special Participation: Verbal recognition during major BBN events.
